✨ Fascinating Star Facts

Stars are massive celestial bodies made primarily of hydrogen and helium that produce light and heat through nuclear fusion. They are the building blocks of galaxies and the source of most elements in the universe.

🌡️ Temperature

Stars range from 2,500°C (red dwarfs) to over 40,000°C (blue giants)

⏳ Lifespan

Small stars live trillions of years, massive stars only millions

💫 Birth

Stars form in giant clouds of gas and dust called nebulae

☀️ Our Sun

A medium-sized yellow dwarf star, 4.6 billion years old

🔴 Red Giants

Dying stars that expand and cool down

💥 Supernovae

Massive stellar explosions that create heavy elements
